Community Rules

Our guidelines for a respectful, safe, and generous community

Table of Contents
Core Principles Giving & Asking Prohibited Items Communication Moderation Reporting Issues Privacy & Safety Group-Specific Rules

Core Principles

The Buy Nothing Project is built on the following core principles:

Give Freely

All items and services must be given freely without any expectation of rewards, trades, or compensation.

Build Community

Our goal is to build stronger, more connected local communities through sharing.

Reduce Waste

By giving items a second life, we reduce waste and environmental impact.

Respect & Kindness

Treat all community members with respect, kindness, and understanding.

Giving & Asking

Guidelines for Giving:
  • All items must be given freely with no expectation of payment, trades, or favors.
  • Be clear about the condition of items, including any defects or issues.
  • Include photos when possible to help recipients understand what you're offering.
  • You may choose recipients in any way you wish (first come, random selection, etc.).
  • Once you've committed to giving an item to someone, please follow through.
  • If an item is no longer available, update or delete your post promptly.
Guidelines for Asking:
  • Be specific about what you're looking for and why if relevant.
  • It's okay to ask for anything (within the prohibited items guidelines).
  • Be respectful if your request cannot be fulfilled.
  • If you no longer need an item, update or delete your post.
Services & Lending:
  • Services (like help moving, tutoring, etc.) can be offered freely.
  • Items can be lent temporarily with clear expectations about return.
  • Professional services may be offered, but not advertised as a business.

Prohibited Items

The following items are not allowed to be given or requested:

Illegal items or substances
Weapons or dangerous items
Prescription medications
Alcohol or tobacco products
Recalled or unsafe items
Adult content or services
Expired food items
Used personal hygiene items
Important: Items that violate these guidelines will be removed, and repeated violations may result in account suspension.

Communication

Community Interaction Guidelines:
  • Be respectful and kind in all communications.
  • No hate speech, harassment, or bullying of any kind.
  • Avoid political, religious, or controversial discussions.
  • Respect others' time and commitments.
  • If you can't make a scheduled pickup, let the giver know as soon as possible.
  • Thank people for their generosity.
Messaging Guidelines:
  • Keep personal information private until necessary for exchange.
  • Be clear about pickup/delivery arrangements.
  • Report any suspicious or uncomfortable messages to moderators.
  • Do not use the messaging system for solicitation or harassment.

Moderation

Moderator Roles:
  • Moderators are volunteers who help maintain community standards.
  • They review reported content and enforce community guidelines.
  • Moderators can remove inappropriate content and issue warnings.
  • In serious cases, they may temporarily or permanently suspend accounts.
Consequences for Rule Violations:
Violation First Offense Second Offense Third Offense
Minor Rule Violation Warning 24-hour suspension 7-day suspension
Major Rule Violation 7-day suspension 30-day suspension Permanent ban
Severe Violation (harassment, illegal activity) Immediate permanent ban

Reporting Issues

How to Report:
  1. Click the "Report" button on any post or comment that violates guidelines.
  2. Select the appropriate reason for reporting.
  3. Add any additional details that might help moderators understand the issue.
  4. Submit the report.
What to Report:
  • Prohibited items or services
  • Harassment or bullying
  • Spam or commercial content
  • Inappropriate or offensive content
  • Safety concerns
  • Scams or suspicious activity
For urgent issues or concerns about personal safety, please contact your local authorities directly.

Privacy & Safety

Personal Information:
  • Never share your full address publicly in posts or comments.
  • Share personal contact information only through private messages.
  • Be cautious about sharing personal details even in private messages.
  • Never share financial information with other users.
Meeting Safety:
  • For first-time exchanges, consider meeting in public places.
  • Let someone know when and where you're meeting someone.
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, cancel the exchange.
  • Be especially cautious when inviting strangers to your home or visiting theirs.
Safety First: Your personal safety is more important than any item or exchange. Never feel pressured to complete an exchange if you feel uncomfortable.

Group-Specific Rules

In addition to the platform-wide rules, individual groups may have their own specific guidelines based on local needs and preferences. These might include:

  • Geographic boundaries for membership
  • Specific days for certain types of posts
  • Local customs or practices
  • Additional restrictions based on community needs
Always check your local group's specific rules when joining a new community. These can usually be found in the group's description or pinned posts.

Agree to Community Rules

By using the Buy Nothing platform, you agree to follow these community guidelines and rules.
