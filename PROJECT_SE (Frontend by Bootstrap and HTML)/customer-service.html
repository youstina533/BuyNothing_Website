Account & Profile

Creating an account on Buy Nothing is simple:

  1. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the page
  2. Enter your email address and create a password
  3. Alternatively, you can sign up using your Google or Facebook account
  4. Complete your profile information, including your name and location
  5. Agree to the community guidelines
  6. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox

Once your account is created, you'll be able to join local groups and start participating in the Buy Nothing community.

If you've forgotten your password, you can reset it by following these steps:

  1. Click the "Login" button in the top right corner of the page
  2. Click the "Forgot Password?" link below the login form
  3. Enter the email address associated with your account
  4. Check your email for a password reset link
  5. Click the link and follow the instructions to create a new password

If you don't receive the password reset email within a few minutes, check your spam folder or try again.

You can update your profile information at any time:

  1. Click on your profile picture in the top right corner of the page
  2. Select "My Profile" from the dropdown menu
  3. Click the "Edit Profile" button
  4. Update your information as needed
  5. Click "Save Changes" to apply your updates

You can update your name, profile picture, bio, and other information. Note that changing your location may affect which groups you can join.

Groups & Community

Finding and joining a local Buy Nothing group is easy:

  1. Click on "Find Groups" in the navigation menu
  2. Enter your location or allow the site to use your current location
  3. Browse the list of groups near you
  4. Click on a group to view more details
  5. Click the "Join Group" button to request membership
  6. Group admins will review your request and approve it if you meet the group's criteria (usually based on location)

Once approved, you'll be able to see posts from the group and create your own posts to give, ask, or lend items.

If there isn't a Buy Nothing group in your area, you can start one:

  1. Click on "Find Groups" in the navigation menu
  2. Search for your location to confirm there isn't already a group
  3. Click the "Start a Group" button
  4. Fill out the group creation form with details about your location and community
  5. Agree to serve as an admin for the group
  6. Submit your request

Our team will review your request and provide guidance on setting up and managing your new group. As a group admin, you'll be responsible for approving new members, moderating posts, and helping to build a positive community.

Yes, you can join multiple Buy Nothing groups. This might be useful if:

  • You live near the border of two group areas
  • You spend significant time in multiple locations (e.g., home and work)
  • You're moving and want to join your new neighborhood group

However, we encourage members to primarily participate in their immediate local community to build stronger neighborhood connections. Some groups may have specific rules about membership in multiple groups, so please check each group's guidelines.

Giving & Receiving

To create a post offering an item:

  1. From your dashboard or group page, click the "Create Post" button
  2. Select "Give" as the post type
  3. Add a title and description for your item
  4. Upload photos of the item (recommended)
  5. Specify pickup details (location, availability, etc.)
  6. Select which group(s) to post in
  7. Click "Post" to publish your offer

Be clear about the condition of the item and any specific details about pickup. You can choose how to select a recipient (first come, random selection, etc.) and update your post when the item has been claimed.

There are two ways to request items:

Responding to a GIVE post:
  1. Comment on the post expressing your interest
  2. Wait for the giver to select you as the recipient
  3. Arrange pickup details through private messages
Creating an ASK post:
  1. From your dashboard or group page, click the "Create Post" button
  2. Select "Ask" as the post type
  3. Describe what you're looking for
  4. Add any relevant details (size, condition, urgency, etc.)
  5. Select which group(s) to post in
  6. Click "Post" to publish your request

Be specific about what you're looking for, but also be patient. Not all requests can be fulfilled immediately.

The following items are prohibited on Buy Nothing:

  • Illegal items or substances
  • Weapons or dangerous items
  • Prescription medications
  • Alcohol or tobacco products
  • Recalled or unsafe items
  • Adult content or services
  • Expired food items
  • Used personal hygiene items

Additionally, any posts that involve selling, bartering, or monetary exchanges are not allowed. Buy Nothing is strictly for gifting and lending.

For a complete list of prohibited items, please refer to our Community Rules.

Safety & Privacy

Here are some safety tips for exchanging items:

  • Meet in public places for first-time exchanges, especially with users you don't know
  • Bring a friend if you're meeting someone new or going to an unfamiliar location
  • Share your plans with a friend or family member, including where you're going and when
  • Exchange during daylight hours when possible
  • Trust your instincts - if something feels wrong, cancel the exchange
  • Keep personal information private until necessary for the exchange
  • Use the messaging system within the platform rather than sharing personal contact information initially

Remember that your safety is more important than any item or exchange. Never feel pressured to complete an exchange if you feel uncomfortable.

We take your privacy seriously:

  • Your full address is never publicly visible to other users
  • Your contact information is only shared with users you choose to message
  • You control what personal information appears on your profile
  • Group membership is limited to verified users in your local area
  • Our platform uses encryption to protect your data
  • We never sell your personal information to third parties

For more details on how we collect, use, and protect your information, please review our Privacy Policy.

If you ever feel unsafe during an exchange:

  1. Trust your instincts and leave the situation immediately if possible
  2. Contact local authorities if you feel threatened or in danger
  3. Report the user through our platform using the "Report" button
  4. Block the user to prevent further communication
  5. Contact our support team with details of the incident

Your safety is our top priority. We encourage you to cancel any exchange that makes you uncomfortable, and we will support your decision to do so.

Reporting Issues

To report inappropriate content or behavior:

  1. Find the post, comment, or user profile you want to report
  2. Click the three dots (⋮) or "More" button
  3. Select "Report" from the dropdown menu
  4. Choose the appropriate reason for reporting
  5. Add any additional details that might help our moderators understand the issue
  6. Submit the report

Reports are reviewed by our moderation team, who will take appropriate action according to our community guidelines. Your report will remain anonymous to the reported user.

After you submit a report:

  1. Our moderation team reviews the report, usually within 24-48 hours
  2. We investigate the reported content or behavior
  3. If we find a violation of our community guidelines, we take appropriate action, which may include:
    • Removing the content
    • Issuing a warning to the user
    • Temporarily suspending the user's account
    • Permanently banning the user in severe cases
  4. You may receive a notification that your report has been reviewed, but for privacy reasons, we may not be able to share specific details about actions taken

We take all reports seriously and strive to maintain a safe, respectful community for all users.

If you encounter a technical issue or bug:

  1. Click on the "Help" link in the footer or navigation menu
  2. Scroll to the "Contact Support" section
  3. Fill out the support form, selecting "Technical Issue" as the category
  4. Provide as much detail as possible, including:
    • What you were trying to do
    • What happened instead
    • Your device and browser information
    • Screenshots if available
  5. Submit the form

Our technical team will investigate the issue and respond as soon as possible. Your feedback helps us improve the platform for everyone.

Volunteering

To become a Buy Nothing volunteer:

  1. Visit our Volunteer page
  2. Review the available volunteer roles and responsibilities
  3. Fill out the volunteer application form
  4. Specify which role(s) you're interested in
  5. Share your relevant experience and motivation
  6. Submit your application

Our volunteer coordinator will review your application and contact you to discuss next steps. Volunteer opportunities include group admin, community builder, support volunteer, and translation volunteer roles.

Group admins are vital to the Buy Nothing community. Their responsibilities include:

  • Reviewing and approving new member requests
  • Moderating posts and comments to ensure they follow community guidelines
  • Answering questions from group members
  • Resolving conflicts or issues within the group
  • Fostering a positive, inclusive community atmosphere
  • Helping new members understand how Buy Nothing works
  • Collaborating with other admins and the Buy Nothing team

Group admins typically spend 3-5 hours per week on these activities. We provide training and ongoing support for all admins.

All Buy Nothing volunteers receive comprehensive training:

  • Orientation: Introduction to the Buy Nothing philosophy, values, and community guidelines
  • Role-specific training: Detailed guidance on your specific volunteer role
  • Platform training: How to use the admin tools and features
  • Conflict resolution: Strategies for handling disagreements and difficult situations
  • Community building: Tips for fostering an inclusive, active community

In addition to initial training, volunteers have access to ongoing support, regular check-ins with the volunteer coordinator, and a community of other volunteers to share experiences and best practices.
